## Project: Familia

### Situation

Familia wanted to launch a Solana NFT drop that could handle intense mint demand without congestion, protect buyer trust through fair distribution, and keep deployment economics efficient enough for a sustainable release.

### Task

Deliver the full technical foundation for a high-volume Solana drop, covering asset generation, mint mechanics, anti-bot controls, storage, royalties, and wallet-connected buyer experience.

### Action

- Built the PFP generation pipeline with the HashLips art engine
- Used Metaplex Core for standardized NFT creation
- Implemented a custom Candy Machine V2 flow for phased allowlist and public sale mechanics
- Added token-gated anti-bot protection around mint access
- Stored metadata on Arweave for cost-efficient decentralized asset hosting
- Enabled real-time royalty tracking across secondary markets
- Delivered a React frontend with Phantom wallet integration

### Result

- NFTs sold with zero failed transactions
- Achieved materially lower mint costs than comparable Ethereum-based launches
- Gave the Familia team a production-ready Solana launch flow built for scale and buyer confidence

## Project: Svenir

### Situation

Svenir wanted to open its NFT drop to a broader audience by accepting both crypto and credit card payments. That meant removing wallet setup friction for new buyers while still delivering assets quickly and maintaining compliance.

### Task

Build a hybrid NFT purchase flow that supported crypto-native users and fiat-first users in the same launch experience, without sacrificing delivery speed, operational control, or compliance.

### Action

- Integrated Crossmint to support credit card payments
- Built a dynamic NFT minting and escrow flow for fiat purchases
- Released NFTs to the buyer's wallet after purchase completion
- Added gasless onboarding with temporary custodial wallets for new users
- Enabled one-click migration from custodial onboarding to self-custody

### Result

- Half of sales came from credit card users, expanding the drop beyond the crypto-native audience
- Delivered a fast purchase and fulfillment experience across both payment methods
- Maintained full compliance throughout the onboarding and purchase flow
