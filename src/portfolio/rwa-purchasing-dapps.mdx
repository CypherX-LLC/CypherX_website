## Project: Novem

### Situation

Novem needed a purchasing platform that would let customers buy tokenized precious metals with crypto or bank transfer, keep pricing accurate in real time, and provide a smooth wallet-connected experience across devices.

### Task

Build a decentralized purchasing application that connected token vault operations, pricing, payments, wallet support, and admin controls into one user-facing platform.

### Action

- Built a purchase flow that allowed users to buy tokens from a token vault with bank transfer settlement
- Locked precious metal pricing during the purchase process to prevent pricing drift
- Released purchased tokens after confirmed bank transfer receipt
- Created liquidity pools to support DEX swapping
- Added real-time precious metal pricing to trading and purchase workflows
- Delivered multi-wallet support across web3 and mobile wallets, including in-app transaction visualization and portfolio tracking
- Built an admin panel for contract operations such as minting and burning

### Result

- Increased customer acquisition by broadening access to tokenized asset purchases
- Enabled 24/7 trading of tokenized assets
- Expanded market reach

### Key Innovations:

- Hybrid fiat/crypto settlement
- Dynamic pricing adjustments
- Multi-signature escrow releases
