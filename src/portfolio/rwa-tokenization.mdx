## Project: Novem

### Situation

Novem needed a tokenization platform for precious metals that could prove 1:1 asset backing, integrate with its existing operational infrastructure, give investors transparent visibility, and satisfy strict regulatory requirements.

### Task

Build a compliant asset tokenization solution that connected on-chain issuance with real-world reserve verification, investor reporting, and jurisdiction-specific controls.

### Action

- Developed asset-backed token contracts with on-chain proof-of-reserves mechanics
- Integrated daily reconciliation between token supply and vault holdings
- Added KYC and AML screening into the platform workflow
- Implemented jurisdiction-specific rule enforcement and audit-ready reporting
- Built an investor portal for real-time asset verification and transaction history

### Result

- Processed \$150M+ in tokenized assets
- Increased market liquidity by 300%
- Reduced settlement time from days to minutes
- Maintained 100% audit compliance
- Onboarded 25+ institutional investors
- Supported 5+ regulatory jurisdictions

### Key Innovations:

- Hybrid on/off-chain asset verification
- Regulatory KYC/AML integration
